Conduct Simple Seasoned Fries with Cheese Making at Home


| About at a Glance Seasoned Fries with Cheese



The beauty of Seasoned Fries with Cheese not only in the delicious taste, but also in the perfect combination of ingredients and eye-catching colors. This dish is perfect for those who want to enjoy a delicious and mouthwatering snack.

Seasoned Fries with Cheese are a popular and delicious snack with a long root. This dish can be adapted with various spices and cheeses to suit personal tastes.

Popularity: Seasoned Fries with Cheese is a popular street food worldwide and is also enjoyed worldwide. It is often served in restaurants and can be made at home. And :

  • Delicious taste
  • Unique texture
  • Easy to process
  • Suitable for many occasions
  • Reasonable price

| Recipe and How To Cook

1. About at a Glance Recipe

  • Potato: Potatoes are a good source of Carbohydrates, Protein, Fiber, and Vitamin C and also contain a number of other vitamins and minerals, including Vitamin B6, Potassium, and Magnesium.

  • Cheese Powder: Cheese Powder has the characteristic fatty salty taste of cheese, often with the addition of a light sweet flavor and a strong aroma.

  • Deep-fried Dough: Deep-fried dough has a smooth, light texture that dissolves easily in water. When mixed with other ingredients, Deep-fried dough creates a crispy, golden crust for the dish.

2. How To Cook

_ Cooking Ingredients Include:
  • 500g potatoes
  • 100g cheese powder
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 100ml cooking oil
_ How Do It:
Prepare the potatoes:
  1. Peel the potatoes, wash them thoroughly, and cut them into strips about 1 cm long and 0.5 cm thick.
  2. Soak the potatoes in cold water for about 15 minutes to remove excess starch, which will help the potatoes become crispier.
  3. Remove the potatoes from the water and drain them.

Fry the potatoes:
  1. Put the fried potatoes in a large bowl.
  2. Add the cheese powder, cornstarch, salt, and pepper to the bowl of potatoes.
  3. Cover the bowl and shake it well to coat the potatoes evenly with the spices.

Enjoy:
  1. Serve the cheese fries on a plate and enjoy.

Tips:
  1. It is best to choose potatoes of the same size so that they cook evenly.
  2. You can fry the potatoes twice for extra crispiness. The first time, fry the potatoes briefly at a low temperature, and the second time, fry them until golden brown at a high temperature.
  3. You can substitute cheese powder with paprika, garlic powder, or onion powder to create different flavors.
  4. You can add other toppings such as ketchup, chili sauce, or mayonnaise to enhance the flavor of the dish.
