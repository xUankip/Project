Conduct Simple Banana Oatmeal Bread Making at Home

| About at a Glance Banana Oatmeal Bread

Banana Oat Bread is a delicious, nutritious and easy-to-make pie. The cake is made from the main ingredients are oats, ripe bananas and other simple ingredients such as baking powder, chicken eggs, milk, honey ,... The cake has a natural sweetness from ripe bananas, combined with the fatty taste of oats to create a unique and attractive flavor.

Advantage:

  • Easy to make: Banana Oat Bread has a simple way to make it, without many complicated cooking techniques.
  • Nutritious: The cake contains a lot of fiber, vitamins and minerals from Oats and Bananas, good for health.
  • Suitable for many diets: The cake can be modified to suit many diets such as eat clean, keto,...
  • Delicious: The cake has a sweet, delicious taste, suitable for all ages.

| Recipe and How To Cook

1. About at a Glance Recipe

  • Rice Cake (Tteok): The type of rice cake commonly used to make Tokbokki is a cylindrical rice cake made from glutinous rice flour.

  • Spicy Sauce: The spicy sauce is made with Gochujang (Korean paprika), Gochugaru (Korean chili paste), sugar, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger.

  • Toppings: Popular toppings for Tokbokki include fish cakes (Eomuk), boiled eggs, green onions, and roasted sesame.

2. How To Cook

_ Cooking Ingredients Include:
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 190g oatmeal (rolled oats or quick oats)
  • 60ml coconut oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 3 tablespoons honey (or sugar)
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g shredded coconut (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powde
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 8g vanilla sugar
  • 2g cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 50g walnuts, almonds, cashews (optional)
_ How Do It:
Preparation:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Line a bread pan with parchment paper.
Mixing the dry ingredients:
  1. Mash the bananas with a fork.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the oats, baking powder, baking soda, vanilla sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the coconut oil, honey, and eggs.
Mixing the batter:
  1. Add the mashed bananas to the egg mixture and whisk until well combined.
  2. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  3. Fold in the shredded coconut and nuts (optional).
Baking:
  1. Pour the batter into the prepared bread pan.
  2. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  3. Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing.
Enjoy:
  1. Slice the banana oatmeal bread and enjoy with tea, milk, or coffee.
Tips:
  1. You can substitute the shredded coconut with other nuts like almonds, walnuts, or cashews.
  2. You can add 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract to the batter for extra flavor.
  3. If you want a sweeter bread, you can add more honey or sugar to taste.
  4. It is best to use ripe bananas for a naturally sweeter and more flavorful bread.
  5. You can check if the bread is done by inserting a toothpick into the center; if it comes out clean, the bread is done.
  6. Banana oatmeal bread can be stored in an airtight container at room temperature for 3-4 days or in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
