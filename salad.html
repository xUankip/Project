Dish Name: Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Salad

Ingredients:
  • 200g chicken breast
  • 1 bell pepper (optional)
  • 1 tomato
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1 small red onion
  • 50g romaine lettuce
  • 50g curly lettuce
  • 30g spinach
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1/4 cup roasted almonds (optional) Shredded cheese (optional) Where to buy: Supermarket, grocery store Instructions: Preheat oven to 200°C. Marinate chicken breast with olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Bake chicken for 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through. Cut bell pepper, tomato, cucumber, and onion into bite-sized pieces. Wash and chop romaine lettuce, curly lettuce, and spinach. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt, and black pepper. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss to coat. Top with roasted almonds and shredded cheese (optional). Preparation and Cooking Time: Preparation time: 15 minutes Cooking time: 25 minutes Tips: Choose skinless chicken breast for a lower-fat salad. Bell pepper can be substituted with other vegetables such as broccoli, mushrooms, or carrots. Other nuts such as cashews, hazelnuts, or pumpkin seeds can be added to the salad. Salad should be stored in the refrigerator and consumed within 24 hours. Serves: 2 people Calories and Nutritional Information: Calories: 350 kcal Fat: 20g Protein: 30g Carbohydrates: 20g
